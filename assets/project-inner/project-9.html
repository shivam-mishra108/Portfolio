Project Overwiew

The frontend of our text editor website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We have created a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily navigate through the different editing options. The design is clean and intuitive, ensuring a smooth editing experience.
Our text editor website does not require a backend as it primarily focuses on frontend functionality. All the text editing and formatting operations are performed on the client-side using JavaScript. This allows for quick and responsive editing without the need for server communication.

Our text editor provides a wide range of formatting options to suit your needs. You can apply formatting to selected text or the entire document. Whether you want to make your text bold, italicize it, underline it, or even add strikethrough, our editor has got you covered.
Our text editor allows you to choose from a variety of font colors to make your text visually appealing. You can also select different themes to change the overall look and feel of the editor. Whether you prefer a light or dark theme, we have options to suit your preferences.
